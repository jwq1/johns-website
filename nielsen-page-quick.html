Lean Technology Development

To amplify company impact, I release new technology solutions with humans at their center. My team at Nielsen develops new software products identify human needs through data analysis. However, Nielsen is global, and multitudes of stakeholder requirements complicate technology development.

To ensure my team’s solutions met each stakeholder’s necessities, I would have to release quick, iterative solutions to gather user feedback along the way. I worked with my team to develop new products in an Agile way so that we could get consistent reactions from customers in each region. The rapid iterations afforded us the ability to gain a signal about what new consumer demands arose from each new product. We can test ideas swiftly and make the best solutions for our customers.

I like to use my skills launching new technology products to amplify the impact of support socially-driven organizations.

Details:

My latest product uses evolutionary algorithms to create 1 billion+ new concepts, and test these with consumers. The evolutionary algorithm empowers Nielsen to narrow down an unthinkably large idea space, to only the top three concepts. Clients like P&G are able to take action on these top three concepts and get the new products to consumers.
